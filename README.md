# Pyvilization

## What is this abomination?

`Pyvilization` is another side project that'll die out when something shinier comes my way.

I've been obsessed with the game Civilization V lately again. Unrelated to it, my full-time job generally demanding AI usage, and I'm feeling alienated from the joy of writing code. Just aimless, handcrafted, artisanal, all-the-best ingredients kind of code. The one I worked hard to learn more than 20 years ago.

So, I'm basically creating a Civilization-inspired CLI game written in Python. No pre-planning, just coding stuff with my hands and seeing where it goes. It might turn out into a pile of unusable spaghetti but, hell, for once it's my spaghetti again, and I'm happy just to see it come to fruition.
